How I think

"The best AI product is the one where you can't tell where the machine ends and the human begins to trust it."

01

Customer before code. Every project starts with a customer dossier — who they are, what pain they carry, what success looks like for them.

02

AI assists, humans decide. Every AI product I build has a clear boundary. The machine flags, explains, and suggests. The human owns every consequential decision.

03

Ship something real. A working demo beats a slide deck every time. The discipline of finishing matters as much as the discipline of starting right.

04

Documents before code. System design, data models, tradeoff records. The artifacts produced before building are often worth more than the code itself.
